Caprese Salad

Description

This light, fresh salad is made with fresh mozzarella, cherry tomatoes, and fresh basil. It is the perfect quick and easy accompaniment to any summer meal.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces fresh mozarella cheese, cubed
  • 1 (10 ounce) basket cherrytomatoes
  • 3 tablespoons torn fresh basil leaves
  • q tablespoon olive oil
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Toss together the mozzarella, cherry tomatoes, basil, and olive oil in a large bowl. Season with salt and pepper.
