Pan Fried Sesame Garlic Tofu

Description

It’s a great source of protein and low in fat. It’s so tender too and it soaks up all the flavor of the dish. It’s a great blank slate and super versatile.

Ingredients

  • Tofu
  • Soy sauce
  • Sambal oelek
  • Rice vinegar
  • Honey
  • Sesame Oil
  • Green Onion

Steps

  1. Drain the tofu package
  2. Press the tofu to get the remaing liquid out
  3. Let it press for 30min, but change paper towel after 15min
  4. Coat the tofu in cornstarch
  5. Fry the fofu in the pan for 3-4min on each side
  6. Make a sauce out of all the ingridents and coat the tofu in the sauce
